Performance Portfolio

Jazz Academy Big Band (2019–2023)

Professionally performed as a main member of Denis Melnikov's Jazz Academy Big Band. This is a young Moscow jazz orchestra of the Jazz Academy, directed by world-famous saxophonist and bandleader Igor Butman. The big band became a laureate of international competitions such as the "Lanote Jazz Competition" and "Piano in Jazz", and participated in the first ever big band battle in Russian history.

  • Thad Jones – Us
  • Herbie Hancock – Dolphin Dance

LK Sextet (2021–2023)

Performed as a pianist in the ensemble LK Sextet, a promising group known for original compositions. LK Sextet won 1st place at Russia’s top jazz competition, "Piano in Jazz".

  • Run – LK Sextet
  • Skinny Groove – LK Quintet

Mariia Kovalenko Trio

A personal project led by me. Though currently paused due to studying abroad, the Mariia Kovalenko Trio also won 1st place at the "Piano in Jazz" competition.
